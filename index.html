Project Engineering Co-op

USG Corporation · Baltimore, MD

August 2025 – December 2025 Internship

Biomedical Engineering Intern

Project C.U.R.E. · Chicago, IL

As a Biomedical Engineering Intern at Project C.U.R.E., I was responsible for ensuring the quality, and functionality of donated medical equipment from external healthcare facilities. During this experience, I repaired over 100 pieces of biomedical equipment using standard work instructions. Additionally, I determined the equipment's repair feasibility by identifying missing components, and estimating costs to determine whether refurbishment was justified.

Some of the equipment I worked with includes ICU beds, gurneys, infusion pumps, oxygen concentrators, and electrosurgical units. This experience felt highly rewarding as I contributed to 3 international shipments to under-resourced hospitals in developing countries.

Medical Devices Quality Assurance Humanitarian Aid
July 2025 – December 2025 Research

Student Researcher

Illinois Tech · Chicago, IL

As a Student Researcher at the Illinois Institute of Technology, I conducted research on existing produce slicers and developed a cost-effective yet consumer-friendly design.
