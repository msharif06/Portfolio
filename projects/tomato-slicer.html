Tomato Slicer

Precision food-processing mechanism for uniform, repeatable slicing

Overview

A precision food-processing mechanism designed to produce uniform, repeatable slices of tomatoes and similar soft produce. The project focused on mechanism synthesis, ergonomic grip design, and material selection suitable for food-contact applications.
