Bio-Inspired Quadruped Robot

A four-legged walking robot modeled on biological locomotion principles

Overview

A bio-inspired four-legged robot that mimics mammalian gait patterns. The project integrates servo-driven 3D-printed linkages, inverse kinematics solved in Python, and a gait controller tuned for stable locomotion on flat and uneven terrain.
