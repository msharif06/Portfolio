Sustainable Foam Chair

Structurally optimized seating with sustainability as a primary design constraint

Overview

An environmentally conscious seating design that applies structural optimization and material lifecycle analysis to minimize environmental impact without sacrificing comfort or durability. Load paths were analyzed via FEA and material selection was guided by LCA data.
